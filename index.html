Our story of growth is one of sheer will to spread the love for Chelsea Football Club throughout India. From starting out with 4 people in the first screening in Mumbai back in 2006, we expanded to 20 cities throughout the country by our 10th Anniversary. We are proud to be the biggest organized supporters club in India! Each city has its moderators who organize screenings and other activities for the fans.

About Chelsea India Supporters' Club

We have been officially recognized as a supporters’ club by CFC, and were given the Bronze Tier Membership in 2009. This was followed by the launch of our official website and forum in January, 2010. With the help of social media, we have reached out to and connected with thousands of Blues all over the country. Our social media handles on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram & Snapchat are followed by thousands of blues for updates. Every city also has its own Whatsapp Groups where fans get together & have a whole lot of fun!

The Team

View

In arguably the biggest achievement of our club so far, CISC has been upgraded to the Gold Tier Membership with Chelsea. What followed was our need to have an annual event where Blues from all over the country get together. This gave birth to ‘Blue Arena’ in 2015 which gave an experience to remember for all the fans present. Then came ‘Blue Arena 2.0’ in Mumbai (April 2016), the celebration of our 10th Anniversary in style with fans immediately asking “When will the next Blue Arena be held?”. And we answered with Blue Arena 3 on April 16th 2017 (Pune) in style with a massive response from fans & partners alike.

The members of Chelsea India Supporters’ Club may sometimes have nothing whatsoever in common with each other, apart from their love for our fantastic football club. We are family, in a sense, bonded by the Blue blood that runs through our veins. Together, we preview the game at the weekends, watch it at the screenings and then reflect on the result. In the process, we have found some great friends, individuals with whom we will cherish and share a relationship for a lifetime.
